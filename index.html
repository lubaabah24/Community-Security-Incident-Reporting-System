Core Platform Features

Tools designed to help residents of Ojo LGA report incidents, share evidence, and stay informed about response progress.

Submit details of theft, harassment, vandalism, or suspicious activity in your area using a simple guided form.

Upload Evidence

Emergency Contacts

Track Report Status

Monitor the progress of your report and receive updates from the relevant authorities or response teams.

How It Works

Three simple steps to report and resolve community security incidents in Ojo LGA.

1. Report the Incident

Use the secure form to describe what happened, where it occurred, and when it took place.

2. Review and Dispatch

The report is received by the relevant safety and response teams for assessment and action.

3. Track Resolution

Follow updates, receive confirmation, and stay informed until the matter is addressed.

About the Ojo LGA Security Reporting System

This platform helps residents of Ojo Local Government Area report incidents securely, access emergency support, and stay informed about public safety responses.

Secure and responsive

Our Mission

The system helps residents report security incidents quickly and securely while improving communication with Ojo LGA safety agencies and enabling faster, more coordinated response efforts.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build safer communities in Ojo LGA through technology, transparency, and timely incident reporting that empowers residents and support agencies alike.

Objectives

Easy Incident Reporting

Provide a simple and structured way for residents of Ojo LGA to report incidents from any device.

Evidence Upload

Allow users to attach photos, videos, and documents that strengthen case review.

Emergency Communication

Connect residents to emergency contacts and support channels when urgent action is needed.

Incident Tracking

Enable users to follow the progress of their reports with transparency and updates.

Faster Response

Reduce delays by routing reports through a clear and organized digital workflow for local responders.

Community Safety

Encourage participation and cooperation that strengthens neighborhood safety across Ojo LGA over time.
